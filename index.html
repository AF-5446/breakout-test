Recognizing Common Issues

Mental health issues can often be identified by certain signs and symptoms. Here are some common issues:

Depression

Persistent sadness, loss of interest, changes in appetite, sleep issues, fatigue, and difficulty concentrating.

Anxiety

Excessive worry, restlessness, physical symptoms like sweating and trembling, avoidance, and sleep disturbances.

Stress

Irritability, muscle tension, headaches, stomach issues, and difficulty relaxing.
